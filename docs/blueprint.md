# **App Name**: Mzansi Fire

## Core Features:

- Heat-Indexed Sauce Catalog: A browsable gallery of South African sauces categorized by Scoville levels and distinct regional flavor profiles.
- AI Flavor Matchmaker Tool: A generative AI tool that uses reasoning to recommend sauces based on the specific South African dishes or ingredients a user plans to cook with.
- Artisanal Cart System: A streamlined interface for users to select various bottle sizes and quantities of their favorite hot sauces.
- Scoville Slider Filter: An interactive UI component allowing users to filter products by a precise range of heat levels from 'Cape Mild' to 'Wild Veldt Fire'.
- Order Management Mock: A minimal UI flow for collecting shipping information and confirming the purchase of sauce selections.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Terracotta (#EE612C), reflecting the rich soil of the South African interior and the vibrancy of ripe peppers.
- Background color: Dark Umber Charcoal (#1A1614), a heavily desaturated variant of the primary hue to create a sophisticated, moody backdrop that makes product colors pop.
- Accent color: Raspberry Glaze (#EF426F), an analogous hue that provides high contrast for call-to-action buttons and price highlights.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a high-contrast serif that conveys elegance and artisanal quality; Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif for optimal readability and a touch of warmth.
- Custom illustrative pepper icons that vary in size and stroke weight to indicate varying levels of hotness.
- A spacious, editorial-style grid layout that prioritizes high-resolution photography of the sauces and their natural ingredients.
- Subtle, heat-like ripple effects and slow fades between product views to maintain a premium brand feel.
